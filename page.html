What We Do


We cater for all aspects of the recordning process, wether that's recording, composing, audio/film content. We are here to help. No matter the current state of your project, we can accommodate! We offer recording and production services for singles, EPs, and LPs in all genres, including basics tracking, overdubs, sound design, orchestration, and other production layering techniques (organic and abstract).
