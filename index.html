Reasons to visit us!

We are a brand new up and coming recording studio. We have a modern, state of the art space and equipment Rainwater is set up with high quality recording in mind. Our artists will not need to spend their money on unnecessary and expensive equipment they won't use, as everything that is essential to achieving a clean, great sounding recording can be found in the studio. At the heart of the studio is a Logic Pro audio recording system, with 32 Midus Venus preamps and a great selection of microphones suited for all types of recording.

Our experienced producer has over 10 years experience. He has worked on thousand of tracks in endless genres ranging from classical to heavy metal. He is a professional musician and will treat every track with the utmost care and respect. After all, music is an art and should be treated as such. From writing, to performing, to recording, to mixing and mastering, we are… in the words of Jimi Hendrix… experienced. There is no magic button or plug-in that just works for every recording. Each recording and each song needs to be addressed and attended to on an individual basis.

We cater for all genres whether you're an accoustic sinfer/songwritter or part of a large instrumental band. We've got you covered!
